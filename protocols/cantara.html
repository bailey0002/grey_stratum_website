Cantara

Instrumentum: TTS+

Neural Voice Synthesis

Professional text-to-speech with SSML control. Transform written content into natural-sounding audio with precise control over emphasis, pacing, and prosody.

The Tool

Cantara TTS+ provides direct access to Azure Neural Text-to-Speech with a visual SSML editor. Build professional audio content with granular control over voice parameters—no command line required.

Whether creating voiceovers, narration, or accessible content, Cantara gives you 400+ neural voices across 140+ languages with real-time preview and instant export.

[ CAPABILITIES ]

What It Does

Visual SSML Editor

Point-and-click control over emphasis, breaks, pitch, rate, and prosody—no XML coding required

Neural Voices

Access to 400+ Azure Neural TTS voices with natural intonation and expression

Multi-Format Export

Output to MP3, WAV, OGG, or WebM at configurable quality levels

Real-Time Preview

Hear your SSML modifications instantly before committing to final render

[ SSML CONTROLS ]

Fine-Grained Voice Control

◆ Emphasis — Strong, moderate, or reduced stress on selected words
◆ Prosody — Adjust pitch contour for questions, statements, or custom patterns
◆ Rate — Speed control from x-slow to x-fast with percentage precision
◆ Breaks — Insert pauses from 250ms to 5s for natural phrasing
◆ Pitch — Raise or lower voice pitch with semitone or percentage control
◆ Say-As — Proper pronunciation of dates, numbers, addresses, and more
